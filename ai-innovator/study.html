LEARN & COMPETE: TRIPLE-O MODEL

The Triple-O Learning Model is an innovative educational approach designed by VinUniversity and SKOLA that aims to maximize the learning experience for young students. It seamlessly integrates three core components: Online, Offline, and Office Hours.

Online: This segment involves interactive, digital learning platforms where students can engage with educational content at their own pace. The online component offers a variety of multimedia resources such as videos, quizzes, and interactive modules, making learning both engaging and flexible.

Offline: Here, students are encouraged to engage in hands-on activities and real-world learning experiences. This could include projects, experiments, or field trips, which are crucial for developing practical skills and reinforcing theoretical knowledge. The offline element emphasizes the importance of tangible experiences in education.

Office Hours are dedicated sessions where students can interact with educators and peers. These sessions provide personalized support, allowing students to ask questions, discuss challenges, and explore topics in greater depth. Office Hours foster a sense of community and collaboration, essential for a comprehensive learning experience.

1st-O: Online Learning

Online learning resources offer comprehensive knowledge and skill development, essential for developing proposals in the directions desired by judges

Introduction to AI

Designed for beginners, this course aiming to demystify the world of AI. It covers the fundamentals, including machine learning, neural networks, and their applications in various industries.

The Psychology Of Technology AI

This unique program delves into how AI technologies influence human behavior, decision-making, and societal norms. Participants will explore topics like the psychological impact of AI interactions, and the future of human-AI relationships.

Ethics of AI

This course targets students, tech professionals, and anyone interested in the moral dimensions of artificial intelligence. This course explores the ethical challenges and considerations in AI development and deployment, including bias, privacy, transparency, and the societal impact of AI decisions.

AI And Economics

The course offers an in-depth analysis of how AI is reshaping economic theories, labor markets, financial systems, and global trade. Participants will explore topics such as the impact of AI on productivity, employment, market dynamics, and industry implications.

Idea Generation With AI

The course covers strategic approaches to integrating AI into various business operations. Students will learn about AI-driven analytics, predictive modeling, and automation, gaining insights into how these technologies can enhance efficiency, innovation, and competitive advantage.

2nd-O: Offline Workshops

Offline workshops provide opportunities to meet experts and mentors in person, network and learn from them.

Workshop 01

Information Session

An online webinar to cover all the main information about the competition, tips and tricks to make an outstanding submission, and sharings from AI experts. Students will be able to ask questions to members of the competition committee in an interactive Q&A session at the end.

Workshop 02

AI for everyone

Perfect for individuals of all backgrounds, regardless of their technical expertise, the workshop aims to demystify AI, explaining its core concepts, real-world applications, and potential impact on society in an easy-to-understand manner. Participants will learn about how AI works, its ethical considerations, and its role in everyday life.

Workshop 03

Capitalizing Opportunities with OpenAI

The workshop focuses on practical applications of OpenAI products in various business scenarios, including enhancing customer engagement, streamlining operations, and driving innovation. Participants will gain valuable insights into leveraging AI for strategic advantage, and exploring the future possibilities of AI in the business world.

Workshop 04

Communications in the Age of AI

This event provides insights into how we can convince customers or investors to buy AI products. Attendees will explore the integration of AI in public relations, digital marketing, content creation, and social media strategies. The workshop emphasizes the development of AI-driven communication strategies, ethical considerations, and the future trends of AI in media and communication.

3rd-O:

Office Hours

"Office Hours" forms the third and pivotal component of the Triple-O Learning Model, designed to provide a personalized and interactive learning experience for students. This element offers dedicated time slots where students can connect with educators and peers to discuss course material, clarify doubts, and delve deeper into specific topics.
